    How payout triggers work

    • Claims are initiated against active policies with validated trigger events.
    • Automated fraud scoring routes suspicious claims to review lanes.
    • Low-risk claims can be approved and paid immediately.
    • Duplicate event attempts are blocked through idempotency keys.

    How to keep trust high

    • Keep payout account verified and profile details consistent.
    • Avoid frequent location anomalies and suspicious device behavior.
    • Submit honest requested amounts within realistic weekly loss bands.
    • Use standard plans for balanced premium and payout coverage.
